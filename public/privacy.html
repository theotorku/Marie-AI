Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Marie AI ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

What we collect

  • Account data: email address and password (hashed with bcrypt).
  • Usage data: chat messages, tasks, contacts, and notifications you create in the app.
  • Integration tokens: Google OAuth tokens for Gmail and Calendar access and Slack OAuth tokens, used only to provide the integrations you connect.
  • Billing data: handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card information.

How we use it

  • To deliver Marie AI's features (chat, briefings, agent notifications).
  • To send transactional emails about your account.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not train AI models on your data.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic (Claude): processes your chat messages to generate responses. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Supabase: stores your account and app data (encrypted at rest).
  • Google & Slack: only when you connect these integrations, and only for the scopes you grant.
  • Stripe: processes payments.

Security

API keys never reach your browser; AI calls go through our server. Account access is protected with JWT authentication and server-side per-user checks. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, and integration tokens are stored server-side.

Your rights

You can request export or deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@jmarie.beauty. We respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@jmarie.beauty.
